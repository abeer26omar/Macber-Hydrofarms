About Us

Who We Are ?

Working Since 2012

Hydrofarms is a regional pioneer in Hydroponic farming. A labor of love that started 9 years ago is now a sustainable entity producing more than 7 tons of produce a month, with a rapidly growing customer base across all their products & services. Unlike many freestanding farms, Hydrofarms has a holistic approach integrating many arms of the hydroponic cycle resulting in a sustainable entity that is seamlessly scaling.

Hydrofarms for Agri-solutions was established in 2018, specializing in full integrated agricultural solutions, with the purpose of developing the local and regional agricultural industry and covering its demand for high quality, clean & fresh produce. Hydrofarms uses the application of smart technologies and solutions tailored to the regional environment and market needs.
